## 🚀 3 STEPS TO PLAY

### **1. Add Game Setup**
In Unity:
1. **Hierarchy** → Right-click → **Create Empty**
2. Name it: **`GameSetup`**
3. Select it → **Inspector** → **Add Component**
4. Search: **`CompleteGameSetup`** → Add

### **2. Press Play ▶️**
Click Play button - everything spawns automatically!

### **3. Start Playing!**
- **R** → Choose your race
- **B** → Buy weapons ($800 starting money)
- **WASD** → Move and shoot!
